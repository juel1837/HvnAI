What makes HavenCraft the best Bangladeshi Minecraft server?

Festivals, Reimagined

Your favorite Bangladeshi celebrations — now in Minecraft. Special events for Pohela Boishakh, Eid, and Victory Day with cultural challenges, themed worlds, and exclusive rewards that celebrate our traditions in the Minecraft universe.

Active Bangladeshi Community

Every division. Every district. One incredible Minecraft community. Connect with players from across Bangladesh in our vibrant SMP server. From late-night gaming sessions to community build projects, your next great friendship starts here on this Bangladeshi Minecraft server.

Craft Our Landmarks

Join hundreds of builders bringing Bangladesh to life in Minecraft. From Mughal forts and the Sundarbans to modern marvels like the Padma Bridge, every block celebrates our story. Contribute to national landmark replicas on our Minecraft SMP server.

Grief Protection

Build without fear on our Minecraft server Bangladesh! Our advanced claim system ensures your hard work is protected from griefers while still allowing cooperative community builds. Claim land with a simple golden shovel and build in peace.

Player Economy

Trade, sell, and become the richest player on the server. Engage in a fully player-driven economy with chest shops, auctions, and a thriving marketplace — all within our Bangladeshi Minecraft SMP community.

PvP Arenas

Test your skills against other players in dedicated PvP arenas on HavenCraft. Fight without risking your survival gear — pure skill, zero risk. Compete in tournaments and climb the leaderboard on the best Minecraft server in Bangladesh.

Java & Bedrock Crossplay

HavenCraft supports both Minecraft Java Edition and Bedrock Edition with full crossplay. Connect from PC, mobile, or console — everyone plays together on the same Bangladeshi Minecraft server. IP: hvn.bd, Port: 25666.
