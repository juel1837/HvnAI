Privacy Policy

How we handle your data.

Information Collection

We may collect basic information such as your Minecraft username, IP address, and in-game activity. This information is required to operate the server, ensure fair gameplay, and maintain security.

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used strictly for server-related purposes, including moderation, anti-cheat systems, enforcing bans, and improving gameplay experience. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to any third parties.

Data Retention

We retain player data only for as long as necessary to operate the server and enforce rules. Certain data, such as ban records, may be stored for a longer period to protect the server and its community.

Third Party Services

We may use third-party services such as mc-heads.net (for player avatars) and mcsrvstat.us (for server status). These services operate independently and have their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for their practices.

Data Protection

We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to or deletion of your data. If you wish to make such a request, please contact us through our Discord server.

Children's Privacy

Our server is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the server after changes means you accept the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our Discord server.
