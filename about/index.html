About HavenCraft

Our Story & Vision

Welcome to HavenCraft — where Bangladesh plays Minecraft together. Born from late-night Discord calls and a shared love for our roots, we built a home for gamers who want more than just survival.

Here, Pohela Boishakh means server-wide festivals. Your district has a representative. Your next build might be a replica of your village.

We keep it simple: lag-free SMP, 24/7 uptime, and a community that actually talks. Java or Bedrock — if you bleed green and red, you're one of us.
