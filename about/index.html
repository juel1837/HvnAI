About HavenCraft

Our Story, Mission & Vision for the Bangladeshi Minecraft Community

Welcome to HavenCraft — where Bangladesh plays Minecraft together. Born from late-night Discord calls and a shared love for our roots, we built a home for gamers who want more than just survival. HavenCraft is the premier Bangladeshi Minecraft server, designed from the ground up for players across Bangladesh.

Here, Pohela Boishakh means server-wide festivals. Your district has a representative. Your next build might be a replica of your village. Whether you're from Dhaka, Chittagong, Sylhet, or Rajshahi — there's a place for you in our growing Minecraft SMP Bangladesh community.

We keep it simple: lag-free SMP, 24/7 uptime, and a community that actually talks. Java or Bedrock — if you bleed green and red, you're one of us. With full Java and Bedrock crossplay support, HavenCraft brings all Bangladeshi Minecraft players together on one server.

Our Mission: To create the most welcoming, culturally rich, and technically excellent Minecraft server for Bangladesh. We believe gaming can bring our community together, celebrate our culture, and give every player from Bangladesh a world-class Minecraft experience.

What Sets Us Apart: HavenCraft isn't just another Minecraft server — it's a Bangladeshi Minecraft community. We host regular cultural events, maintain a player-driven economy, offer grief-protected land claims, and operate dedicated PvP arenas. Our staff team is active 24/7 to ensure every player has the best experience on any Minecraft server in Bangladesh.
